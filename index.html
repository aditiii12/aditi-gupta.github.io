About

I am currently a Master’s student in Statistics at the University of Chicago with a strong background in generative AI and statistical modeling. My research centers on diffusion models, multimodal large language models, and uncertainty quantification, where I build end-to-end pipelines to improve robustness, calibration, and interpretability. I have worked on projects spanning multimodal forecasting, LLM uncertainty probing, and high-dimensional covariance modeling, combining rigorous theory with practical, scalable implementations. I have completed my undergrad from IIT Delhi in 2024.
